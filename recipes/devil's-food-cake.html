Devil's Food Cake

This Devil’s Food Cake recipe gives a soft, rich, chocolatey and moist cake. Devil’s Food Cake is a classic for a reason - anyone who likes chocolate will love this!

    Batter Ingredients
  • 140g (1 cup) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 300g (1½ cups) white sugar
  • 100g (1 cup) unsweetened cocoa power
  • 240g (1 cup) milk
  • 180g (¾ cup) sour cream
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp coffee
  • 115g (1 stick) butter
    Frosting Ingredients
  • 80g (¾ cup) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 400g (3¼ cups) powdered sugar
  • 150g (1¼ cups) soft butter, cut into small pieces
  • 120g (½ cup) heavy cream
    Instructions for Cake Batter
  1. Preheat your oven to 320°F (160°C).
  2. Grease, flour and line with parchment paper two 8 inch cake pans. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl combine the flour, baking soda, salt. Whisk and set aside.
  4. In a large deep sauce pan, add the milk and butter. Turn the heat to medium and allow the butter to melt and the mixture to heat up. Do not bring to a boil!
  5. Once the mixure is hot, turn off the flame and whisk in the cocoa powder. Add the coffee and sour cream and combine until fully incorporated. Mix in the sugar until well combined.Now whisk in 2 eggs.
  6. Whisk in the dry ingredients. Whisk until there are no more visible spots of flour. Using a spatula scrape the bottom and sides of your pan.
  7. Divide the cake batter evenly between the two cake pans and bake the cakes for 30 to 35 minutes. Do the toothpick test, if it comes out clean the cakes are ready.
  8. Allow the cakes to cool in their pans for 5 minutes, then turn them over onto a cooling rack. Remove the parchment paper and allow the cakes to cool completely.
    Instructions for Frosting
  1. In a bowl combine powdered sugar, unsweetened cocoa powder and salt. Do not skip the salt.
  2. Add the softened butter cut into little cubes.
  3. Heat your cream and pour over the other ingredients and mix until well combined. The frosting will look lose.
  4. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 45 minutes.
  5. Once cold, whip the mixture using electric beaters before using.
